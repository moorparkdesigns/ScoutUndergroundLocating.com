Our Locating & Inspection Services

Accurate and dependable locating and inspection solutions for underground utilities.

Utility Locating

Accurate tracing of underground utilities.

We use professional UL locators to identify the exact position of underground cables, metallic pipes, and utility lines. This allows for safe and efficient excavation, minimizing risks and costly repairs.

PVC Pipe Tracing

Advanced methods for non-metallic pipe detection.

We specialize in tracing non-metallic pipelines like PVC using advanced techniques such as GPR and internal transmitters. This ensures accurate detection, even when traditional tools fall short.

Depth Detection

Know how deep your utilities run.

We provide detailed depth measurements for underground utilities, giving you full control over your excavation plans. Our systems ensure your team digs safely and within compliance.

Leach Line Inspection

Comprehensive inspection of septic leach lines.

We inspect leach lines using high-definition sewer cameras and GPR to identify blockages, breaks, or misalignments. Our inspections meet county requirements and support permit approvals.
